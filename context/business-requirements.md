# Business Requirements - Beaches Pet Sitting

## Company Overview
- **Name:** Beaches Pet Sitting
- **Industry:** Pet Care Services
- **Service Type:** In-Home Overnight Pet Sitting
- **Experience:** 11+ years professional experience with Veterinary Assistant degree
- **Target Market:** Pet owners who prefer their pets stay comfortable at home while they're away
- **Service Area:** Local area (specific location to be confirmed)

## Unique Value Proposition
- **Professional Credentials:** Degreed Veterinary Assistant with 11+ years experience
- **Comprehensive Care:** All small animals including reptiles
- **Mature & Reliable:** Fully bondable, mature professional service
- **In-Home Comfort:** Pets remain in familiar environment
- **Personalized Attention:** Individualized care focused on safety, health, and TLC

## Services Offered
- In-home overnight pet sitting
- Comprehensive small animal care (including reptiles)
- Personalized pet care routines
- Health and safety monitoring
- Compassionate, knowledgeable care

## Pricing Structure
- **Full Day (24 hours):** $125
- **Half Day (12 hours or portion):** $75
- **Initial Interview:** Free (Always)

## Business Objectives
- **Primary Goal:** Generate qualified leads for initial interviews
- **Conversion Funnel:** Website Visit → Interview Booking → Service Agreement
- **Success Metrics:**
  - Number of interview bookings per month
  - Conversion rate from visit to booking (target: 8-12%)
  - Average time to booking decision (target: <3 minutes)

## Key Messages
- Professional veterinary experience
- Mature, compassionate, and knowledgeable
- Pets stay comfortable at home
- Free initial interview to discuss needs
- Bondable and reliable service
- Client recommendations available

## Stakeholders
- **Business Owner:** Beaches Pet Sitting proprietor
- **Target Customers:** Local pet owners who travel or need pet care
- **Pet Types:** Dogs, cats, small animals, reptiles
- **Decision Factors:** Trust, experience, professional credentials, in-home service

## Constraints & Requirements
- **Technical:** Simple one-page website with embedded booking calendar
- **Budget:** Cost-effective solution using modern frameworks
- **Timeline:** Quick deployment for immediate lead generation
- **Compliance:** Professional service standards, bondability requirements

## Competitive Positioning
- **Differentiators:**
  - Veterinary Assistant degree (vs. basic pet sitters)
  - 11+ years professional experience
  - Reptile care expertise
  - Mature, professional approach
  - In-home overnight services
  - Free consultation

## Customer Journey
1. **Awareness:** Find website through search/referral
2. **Interest:** Read credentials and service description
3. **Consideration:** Review pricing and testimonials
4. **Decision:** Book free interview
5. **Action:** Schedule through calendar
6. **Service:** In-home interview and service agreement

## Website Goals
- Build trust through professional presentation
- Clearly communicate unique qualifications
- Make booking process frictionless
- Showcase expertise and compassion
- Convert visitors to interview bookings

## Call-to-Action Strategy
- **Primary CTA:** "Book Your Free Interview"

  - "Check Availability"
  - "Get Started Today"

## Content Requirements
- Professional bio highlighting credentials
- Service descriptions with benefits
- Clear pricing information
- Booking calendar integration
- Trust signals (experience, bondable, recommendations)

## Emotional Drivers
- **Peace of Mind:** Pets safe with professional care

- **Trust:** Veterinary credentials and experience
