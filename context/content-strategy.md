## Content Voice Guidelines

### Tone Attributes
- **Professional:** Emphasize credentials and experience
- **Warm:** Show genuine care for animals
- **Trustworthy:** Highlight reliability and bondability
- **Knowledgeable:** Demonstrate veterinary expertise
- **Approachable:** Friendly without being overly casual

### Writing Style
- **Sentences:** Clear and concise, mix of lengths
- **Paragraphs:** Short for web reading (2-3 sentences)
- **Voice:** Active voice primarily
- **Person:** First person for bio, second person for benefits

### Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Emphasize professional credentials
- Use specific numbers (11+ years)
- Include trust signals throughout
- Focus on pet comfort and owner peace of mind
- Use warm, caring language

**Don't:**
- Use veterinary jargon
- Make medical claims
- Compare negatively to other services
- Use fear-based messaging
- Overwhelm with too much information
